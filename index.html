Armadillos!

What's an Armadillo?

Armadillos are New World placental mammals in the order Cingulata with a leathery armour shell.
WHAT?
That's right, the order Cingulata. Never seeen an armadillo? No worries, pal, I'll hook you up.

BAM! Ain't she a beaut?

Why Should I Care?

We appreciate your skepticism, but armadillos need our help. While that armored plating may protect against snakes, coyotes, etc., it doesn't do a whole lot of good against cars, trucks, or ATVs.
So please keep an eye out for your little buddy, the armadillo. And thanks for reading.
